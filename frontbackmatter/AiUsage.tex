\chapter*{Usage of Generative AI}
\thispagestyle{empty}
OpenAI GPT-5 and GPT-5.2 (OpenAI, 2025) was used in order to aid in the creation of this thesis as described below.

\noindent OpenAI GPT-5 and GPT-5.2 (OpenAI, 2025) were used in order to find sources and summarize them.

\noindent OpenAI GPT-5 and GPT-5.2 (OpenAI, 2025) were used in order to build both transpilers and the node.js backend server.

\noindent OpenAI GPT-5 and GPT-5.2 (OpenAI, 2025) were used in order to assist in the writing process. Textual information was not generated with the assistance of AI. Rather, it was used to proof read and give the writing an academic style.
