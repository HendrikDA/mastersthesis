\chapter*{Usage of Generative AI}
\thispagestyle{empty}
OpenAI's GPT-5 and GPT-5.2 (OpenAI, 2025) \cite{openai_chatgpt_2023} were used to aid in the creation of this thesis as described below.

\begin{itemize}
	\item In the literature review to help expand search terms and improve search quality by considering additional / similar keywords leading to better search results in Google Scholar and other search engines, as well as by leveraging the "Deep Research" functionality, which conducts a multi-step research on the internet.
	\item To summarize meeting notes, ideas, and protocols while organizing and writing about the Action Research.
 	\item To assist in the general writing process. Factual information was not generated with the assistance of AI. Rather, AI was used to proofread, give feedback and ideas, and support in polishing the paper's academic writing style.
\end{itemize}
